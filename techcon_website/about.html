History of TechCon

TechCon was first launched in 2010 as a small tech gathering. Since then, it has grown into one of the most prominent conferences in the tech industry, attracting thousands of professionals and innovators from around the world. With over a decade of history, TechCon has become the go-to event for discovering the latest trends and innovations in technology.

Notable Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has hosted some of the most influential speakers in the tech world. These experts have shared their groundbreaking ideas, research, and visions for the future of technology. Here are a few of our past speakers:

Elon Musk

Elon Musk, CEO of SpaceX and Tesla, delivered a talk about the future of electric vehicles and space exploration. His bold vision for a sustainable future continues to inspire millions.

Sheryl Sandberg

Sheryl Sandberg, COO of Facebook, shared her insights on leadership, technology, and gender equality in the tech industry. Her talk emphasized the importance of empowering women in tech.

Satya Nadella

Satya Nadella, CEO of Microsoft, talked about the role of AI and cloud computing in shaping the future of business. His innovative approach to tech continues to redefine the industry.
